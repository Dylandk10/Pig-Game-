RULES AND GAMEPLAY

  • Objective: To roll dice and try to obtain highest score
  • Select ending score in center
  • 1. If you roll a one, next players turn.
  • 2. If you roll two six's in a row, next players turn.
  • 3. If you roll double sixes, next players turn.
  • To see what you roll pull up console.log
